WASH for Schools

Clean water, safe sanitation, and hygiene habits for every learner.

We completed groundwork across Magadi and Ramasandra clusters to map every tap, toilet, and hygiene ritual. The result is a donor-ready plan that blends infrastructure upgrades with behaviour change.

Our approach

01. Diagnose

Infrastructure audit, water quality tests, and hygiene behaviour surveys with students, teachers, and SDMC members.

02. Deliver

Local contractors fix toilets, set up hand-wash stations, and install safe disposal units while volunteers run hygiene sprints.

03. Sustain

Student WASH clubs, monthly cleaning drives, and micro-grants ensure assets stay functional and habits stick.

Stories from groundwork

GHPS Bachenahatti

From a single tap to a six-point hand-wash bay.

Students mapped their daily routines and co-designed a hand-wash layout using chalk dust. The final design reduces queue time from 12 minutes to 4.

Ramasandra Cluster

Menstrual health circles led by alumni mentors.

Young women alumni facilitated honest conversations, inspiring the SDMC to budget for reusable pad kits and privacy locks.
