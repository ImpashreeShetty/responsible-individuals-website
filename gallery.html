Inauguration Event

July 19th, 2025

Our NGO initiative was officially inaugurated at GHPS School with a successful Sports Day for government school children. The event encouraged participation, teamwork, and joy among the students.

Highlights: Track events, fun games, and a closing ceremony with refreshments. The event fostered community spirit and responsibility among all participants.

Impact: Created memorable experiences for children and established the foundation for our ongoing community development programs.
