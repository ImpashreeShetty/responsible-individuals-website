Educational & Community Engagement Drive

Month: September & October 2025

Activity:organizing a comprehensive educational and community engagement drive across 25 Government Schools in the Bachenahatti Cluster (Magadi Taluk) and Ramasandra Cluster (Bangalore South). The initiative is beyond distributing stationery and study materials—it is a celebration of learning, creativity, and awareness.

Activities include:

Creative Competitions: Drawing, Debate, and Quiz contests to spark curiosity and confidence among students.

Sports & Games Day: Encouraging teamwork, fitness, and fun.

Health & Hygiene Awareness: Sessions promoting well-being and healthy habits.

Environmental Initiatives: Tree plantation and sapling distribution to nurture a greener tomorrow.

Digital Literacy: Awareness sessions on digital learning platforms to bridge the technology gap.

Sustainability Workshops: Paper bag and paper pen making, along with discussions on the impact of single-use plastics.
